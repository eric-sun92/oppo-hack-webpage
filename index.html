About Us
Our product integrates your in-home devices with OPPO’s sleep-sensing technology in order to protect both your electricity bill and your circadian rhythms. You can rest easy with the knowledge that your sleep cycles are protected by our app.
What is rest easy?
Our Product
RestEasy operates in sync with smart devices such as OPPO’s Watch Live and ColorOS Phone. When one of these devices detects that a user has fallen asleep, RestEasy springs into action, sending the necessary commands to the in-home router to dim the lights, turn off the TV, set the thermostat to the appropriate temperature, and perform other operations. The user can preselect their desired commands using the RestEasy application.
Why rest easy?
Our Vision
In this day and age, technology has become so ubiquitous that it is sometimes easy to overlook its presence in our lives. While the benefits of such innovations are numerous, they do present certain drawbacks: an open laptop can disrupt circadian rhythms; a fluorescent light left on overnight can ratchet up electricity bills. Our mission is to minimize these drawbacks by integrating sleep-sensing technology with users’ in-home devices, enabling a hands-free approach to nighttime routines. In doing so, we hope to make a positive impact on the environment by encouraging a reduction in both light pollution and energy waste.

rest easy - Main Features

Lights
RestEasy allows you to time your home lights to your circadian rhythms. Using our simple app, you can choose when you would like the lights in your home to turn off at night and turn back on in the evening. This technology can also function as a remote light timer, allowing you to protect your home from afar.
